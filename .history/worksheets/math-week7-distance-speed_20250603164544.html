How Robots Think About Movement!

Robots use math to figure out how far to go and how fast to move. They calculate distance and speed just like we do!

Robot Movement Math:

  • Distance: How far the robot needs to travel (feet, meters, steps)
  • Speed: How fast the robot moves (feet per second, steps per minute)
  • Time: How long it takes to get there
  • Path: The route the robot takes
