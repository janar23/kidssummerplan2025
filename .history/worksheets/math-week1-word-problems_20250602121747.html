Problem 1: Apples in the Basket

Sanha has 7 apples in her basket. Sanav gives her 5 more apples. How many apples does Sanha have now?

Answer:

Hint: To solve this problem, add the number of apples Sanha already has (7) to the number of apples Sanav gives her (5).

Step 1: Identify what you know. Sanha has 7 apples, and Sanav gives her 5 more.
Step 2: When someone gets more of something, you add.
Step 3: Calculate 7 + 5 = 12
Step 4: The answer is 12 apples.

Problem 2: Birds on a Tree

There are 8 birds sitting on a tree. 3 birds fly away. How many birds are left on the tree?

Answer:

Hint: When birds fly away, they are leaving the tree, so you need to subtract.

Step 1: Identify what you know. There are 8 birds on the tree, and 3 birds fly away.
Step 2: When something leaves or is taken away, you subtract.
Step 3: Calculate 8 - 3 = 5
Step 4: The answer is 5 birds left on the tree.

Problem 4: Toy Cars

Sanav has 12 toy cars. He gives 5 toy cars to his friend. How many toy cars does Sanav have left?

Answer:

Hint: When Sanav gives away toy cars, he has fewer cars, so you need to subtract.

Step 1: Identify what you know. Sanav has 12 toy cars and gives away 5 of them.
Step 2: When something is given away, you subtract.
Step 3: Calculate 12 - 5 = 7
Step 4: The answer is 7 toy cars left.
