🌟 The Honest Friends 🌟

A Story about Sanav, Sanha, and Sai Vittal

👦🏽👧🏽👦🏽
Three Best Friends
at Somtherman Elementary

Set in Prosper, Texas

A tale about the power of honesty

🏫 Somtherman Elementary School 🏫
Prosper, TX
In the sunny town of Prosper, Texas, at Somtherman Elementary School, lived three best friends: Sanav, Sanha, and Sai Vittal.

Sanav and Sanha were twins who loved to learn and play together. Sai Vittal was their closest friend, and the three of them were inseparable!
2
📚 Math Class 📚
Important Test Day
One morning, their teacher Ms. Johnson announced a surprise math test. All the students groaned, but the three friends had studied hard together the night before.

"Don't worry," whispered Sanha to her brother. "We studied all those addition and subtraction problems together!"
3
🤔 A Difficult Moment 🤔
What should they do?
During the test, Sai Vittal got stuck on a really hard problem. He could see Sanav's paper clearly and noticed that Sanav had already solved the same problem.

Sai Vittal thought to himself: "I could just peek at Sanav's answer. No one would know..."
4
✨ Making the Right Choice ✨
Honesty is the best policy
But then Sai Vittal remembered what his parents and teachers always said about being honest. He took a deep breath and decided to try his best on his own.

"Even if I get it wrong," he thought, "at least I'll know I was honest and tried my best."
5
🌳 Under the Oak Tree 🌳
Sharing with friends
After the test, the three friends sat under their favorite oak tree during recess. Sai Vittal looked worried.

"I think I got that last problem wrong," he admitted to Sanav and Sanha. "I could have copied from Sanav, but I didn't want to cheat."
6
🤝 True Friendship 🤝
Supporting each other
Sanav and Sanha smiled proudly at their friend.

"You did the right thing, Sai Vittal!" said Sanha. "We're proud of you for being honest."

"Yeah!" added Sanav. "Getting a wrong answer honestly is better than getting a right answer by cheating."
7
📋 Test Results 📋
A wonderful surprise!
The next day, Ms. Johnson handed back the tests. Sai Vittal was nervous to look at his grade.

To his surprise, he had gotten the problem right after all! His honest effort and careful thinking had paid off.

"I'm so glad I trusted myself and stayed honest," he said with a big smile.
8
🏠 At Home in Prosper 🏠
Sharing the lesson
That evening, all three friends told their families about what happened. Their parents were very proud of Sai Vittal for choosing honesty over an easy answer.

"Honesty always makes us feel good about ourselves," said Sanha and Sanav's mom. "You three are learning one of life's most important lessons."
9
🌟 The Three Best Friends 🌟
Honest, Happy, and True
From that day forward, Sanav, Sanha, and Sai Vittal always remembered the importance of honesty.

💝 The Moral of the Story 💝

Being honest might sometimes seem harder, but it always makes us feel proud of ourselves and helps us grow into better people. True friends support each other in making good choices!

The End
