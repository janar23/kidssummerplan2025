Garden Math Adventures!

Gardens and outdoor spaces are full of math! We measure length, width, area, and plan how much space plants need.

Garden Measurement Tools:

  • Rulers: Measure small distances in inches or centimeters
  • Yard sticks: Measure longer distances in feet or meters
  • Steps: Count steps to estimate bigger areas
  • Grid counting: Count squares to find area
