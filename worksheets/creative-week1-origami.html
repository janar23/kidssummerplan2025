Step 2: Gather Your Materials! 📋

Tip: Let's make sure you have everything you need for origami success!

No origami paper? Regular paper works too - just cut it into a square!

📦 Materials Needed:

Essential Items:
  • 📄 Square paper (6x6 inches works best)
  • ✋ Your hands for folding
  • 📏 Ruler (optional)
  • ✏️ Pencil for marking (optional)
Fun Extras:
  • 🎨 Colored paper or origami paper
  • 👁️ Googly eyes for decorating
  • 🖊️ Markers for details
  • ✨ Stickers for decoration

Pro Tip: If you don't have square paper, you can make a square from regular paper by folding one corner to the opposite edge and cutting off the extra strip!

Step by step: 1) Take rectangular paper 2) Fold corner to opposite edge 3) Cut off the extra strip 4) Unfold for a perfect square!

Step 3: Learn Basic Origami Folds! 📐

Tip: Now that you've watched the video, let's practice the basic folds that all origami uses!

Practice first: Try these folds on scrap paper before starting your animal!

1

Valley Fold ⬇️

Fold the paper toward you, creating a "valley" or dip in the paper.

⬇️
2

Mountain Fold ⬆️

Fold the paper away from you, creating a "mountain" or peak in the paper.

⬆️
3

Crease Well! 💪

After each fold, press down firmly and run your finger along the fold to make a sharp crease.

👆📄
