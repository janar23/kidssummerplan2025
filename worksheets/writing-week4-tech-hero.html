Writing Worksheet - Week 4

Write about an AI or Tech Hero

Instructions:

Learn about some people who made important contributions to computers and technology. Choose one person who interests you, and write about what makes them a technology hero. You can also create your own tech hero if you prefer!

Ada Lovelace

First computer programmer who worked on an early computing machine in the 1840s.

Alan Turing

Created machines to decode messages during World War II and helped develop early computers.

Grace Hopper

Invented one of the first computer programming languages and found the first computer "bug".

Writing Prompt:

My Technology Hero!

Ideas to include:

  • Who is your tech hero? (Choose one from above or make up your own)
  • What did they invent or discover?
  • How did their work help people?
  • Why do you think they are important?
  • If you could ask them a question, what would you ask?
