Step 1: Safety First & Setup! 🛡️

🚨 Important Safety Rules:

  • Always have a grown-up nearby to help
  • Wear old clothes or an apron
  • Only use washable, non-toxic finger paints
  • Wash hands before and after painting
  • Keep paint away from your mouth and eyes

📦 Materials You'll Need:

Essential Supplies:
  • 🎨 Washable finger paints (red, blue, yellow)
  • 📄 Large paper or cardboard
  • 💧 Bowl of water for cleaning fingers
  • 🧽 Paper towels or wet wipes
  • 👕 Apron or old clothes
Optional Fun Extras:
  • 🌟 Glitter (with grown-up help)
  • 🧽 Small sponges for texture
  • 🍃 Leaves for printing
  • 📋 Cardboard for easy cleanup

Step 3: Master Finger Techniques! 👆

Tip: Your fingers are amazing painting tools! Let's learn different ways to use them.

Remember: Different parts of your hand make different marks!

🖇️ Finger Tip Dots

Use just your fingertip to make small dots and circles.

👆 Finger Dragging

Drag your finger to make lines, waves, and squiggles.

🖐️ Palm Printing

Use your whole hand to make big shapes and prints.

🌪️ Finger Swirling

Make circular motions to create swirls and spirals.

Pro Tip: Clean your finger between colors to keep them bright and clear!

How to clean: Dip in water, wipe on paper towel, then start with your new color!
