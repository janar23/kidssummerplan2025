Writing Worksheet - Week 2

Write about Robots

Instructions:

Write about what you know about robots. What do they look like? What can they do? Have you seen any robots? Would you like to have a robot friend? Why or why not?

Helpful Robot Words:

  • robot
  • machine
  • computer
  • metal
  • electronic
  • helper
  • smart
  • buttons
  • screen
  • lights
  • battery
  • program
  • control
  • move
  • speak
  • listen
  • learn
  • work
  • help
  • future

Writing Prompt:

All About Robots!

Ideas to include:

  • What do robots look like?
  • What can robots do to help people?
  • Have you seen robots in movies or books?
  • If you could design a robot, what would it do?
  • Would you like to have a robot friend? Why or why not?
