Writing Worksheet - Week 1

Write about your weekend

Instructions:

Write about what you did during your weekend. Include details about where you went, who you were with, what activities you did, and how you felt. Try to write at least 5-6 sentences.

Helpful Words:

  • Saturday
  • Sunday
  • morning
  • afternoon
  • evening
  • first
  • next
  • then
  • finally
  • played
  • visited
  • watched
  • went
  • saw
  • enjoyed
  • fun
  • exciting
  • interesting
  • happy
  • favorite

Writing Prompt:

My Weekend Adventures

Ideas to include:

  • What day did your weekend start?
  • What was the first thing you did?
  • Did you go anywhere special?
  • Who was with you?
  • What was the most fun part?
  • How did you feel?
